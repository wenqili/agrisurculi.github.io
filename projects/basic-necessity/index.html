Basic Necessity

When it comes to portfolio, my first thought is to be efficient. However, after a lengthy research on website templates, I decided to write my own. I named this website basic necessity.


I was very bad at keeping the habit of editing my documentations. I needed a portfolio mainly for job hunting. The anxiety of getting a job and making money pressures me every day after graduation. I want to use this website as an opportunity to portrait my feeling of torn up between art and bread.


My inspiration is from the contradiction between the gorgeous view from my apartment and my anxiety.
