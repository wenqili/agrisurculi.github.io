Granate Sapian

virtual reality experience

Imagine the universe is a pomegranate. Every seed in this pomegranate is a different world and setting.


In each world, the players start in the smallest scale space. The players have the ability to destroy any object they encounter.


After every 10 seconds they are forced to zoom out the next scale. Eventually the seeds and pomegranate reveal and when the pomegranate closes, the players are pushed to where they started.


However, the players will find out that the damages that they have done stays and their own avatar has been reproduced.This cycle will continue until the current world is either destroyed or fully occupied.
