Grown Into You

360 video diary/letter
  • Works
  • About

I made two sculpture portraits of my parents, and documented my day after thanksgiving as background. This project is a poetic documentary, a visual letter from me to my parents.


2017 marks the tenth year of me living by myself in the US. Tied up by work and life, my parents and I haven't seem each other in six years. I realized recently, wether I like it or not, I have grown more and more like my parents in daily life.
